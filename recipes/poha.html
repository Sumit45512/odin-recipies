Poha

Description

Poha or Kanda Poha is a quick and healthy Indian breakfast dish made with flattened rice. It's perfect for busy mornings when you need something filling yet easy to prepare!

ingredients

  • oil
  • tomato
  • onion
  • green chillies
  • peanuts
  • poha

Steps

  1. Rinse Poha and chop veggies
  2. roast peanuts on medium flame and take them out
  3. Saute the veggies
  4. Add Rinsed poha and mix well
  5. Add salt, turmeric and peanuts
  6. Serve and enjoy!
