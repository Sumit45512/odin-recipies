Samosa

Description

Nobody can resist these perfect pyramids of flaky pastry, stuffed with a delicious spiced potato filling! But not all samosas are created equal. Too dry, not enough filling, bad pastry, weak spicing….. So I set out to make my own, and am proud to now share with you my very own Samosa recipe – my idea of the perfect Samosa!

Ingridients

  • Potato
  • all purpose flour
  • green chillies
  • oil
  • Masala

Steps

  1. boil the potatoes, peel them and mash them
  2. roast the mashed potatoes with masala and green chillies
  3. Kneed the all purpose flour into dough and roll out balls to 2mm thick
  4. cut the discs in the middle with a knife and add the mashed potato filling to create Samosa parcel
  5. Deep fry these parcels unitil golden brown
  6. Serve and enjoy!
